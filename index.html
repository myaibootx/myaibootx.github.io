Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500 g Mascarpone
  • 300 ml Heavy Cream
  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • 100 g Sugar
  • 250 g Ladyfingers
  • 300 ml Espresso
  • 20 g Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla

Instructions

  1. Prepare fresh espresso and let it cool completely.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add mascarpone and vanilla.
  4. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  5. Fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  6. Dip ladyfingers quickly into the espresso.
  7. Create alternating layers of ladyfingers and cream.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours.
  9. Dust generously with cocoa powder before serving.

⭐ Chef's Tip

For the best flavor, prepare the tiramisu the day before serving. The overnight chill allows the coffee, mascarpone, and cocoa to blend beautifully.
